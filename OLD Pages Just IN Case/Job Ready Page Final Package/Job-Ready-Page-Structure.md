## Key Features

### Interactive Elements
1. **Spotify Embed** - Visitors can listen immediately without leaving the page
2. **Subscribe Buttons** - Direct links to Spotify and Apple Podcasts in hero
3. **Explore Buttons** - Link to full episode archives and roundtables
4. **CTA Buttons** - Drive to demo request and EFFA solutions

### Design Consistency
- ✅ Hero matches For Employers/Universities layout pattern
- ✅ Section tags for visual hierarchy (orange and blue)
- ✅ Consistent button styling throughout
- ✅ Footer exactly matches other pages
- ✅ Responsive design for all screen sizes

### Content Flow Strategy
1. **Hero** - Immediate introduction and subscription options
2. **Latest Episode** - Instant listening access (engagement)
3. **About** - Context and credibility (2 years, 35+ episodes)
4. **Hosts** - Personal connection with leadership team
5. **Roundtables** - Premium content showcase
6. **Episodes** - Topic variety demonstration
7. **CTA** - Convert to EFFA platform exploration

### Mobile Responsive
- Hero text scales appropriately
- Spotify embed scales to fit mobile screens
- Host grid stacks to single column
- Footer reorganizes to 2 columns then 1 column
- All buttons expand to full width on mobile

---

**This structure successfully:**
- Engages visitors with immediate listening options
- Establishes credibility through host credentials
- Showcases premium roundtable content
- Funnels visitors to EFFA platform
- Maintains design consistency across site
- Provides excellent mobile experience
