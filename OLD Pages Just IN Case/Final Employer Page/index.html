Where Traditional Tuition Assistance Fails

Your tuition benefit should drive retention and build skills. Instead, it creates administrative burden and compliance risk while employees can't even afford to use it.

The Current Reality

  • Employees can't afford upfront costs—even with 100% reimbursement
  • Manual receipt and transcript verification drains HR time
  • Risk of paying for failed courses or policy violations
  • Multiple invoices from different schools create chaos
  • No visibility into program ROI or retention impact
  • Fraud opportunities through employee-submitted documents

The EFFA Solution

  • Direct payment eliminates employee out-of-pocket burden
  • Automated grade verification—zero manual processing
  • Payment deferred until course completion and compliance
  • One consolidated monthly invoice for all schools
  • Real-time dashboard showing retention and skill impact
  • Institutional reporting eliminates fraud risk entirely
