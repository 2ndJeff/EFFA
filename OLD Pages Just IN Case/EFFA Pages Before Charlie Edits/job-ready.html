ABOUT THE PODCAST

Exploring the Path from Education to Employment

Job Ready is the EFFA bi-weekly podcast dedicated to the issues that universities, their students, and the employers who hire them face around creating job readiness within the next generation of the US workforce.

Kitchen-Table Conversations with Industry Leaders

Informed by the issues you're grappling with today, join us bi-weekly to hear how innovators and entrepreneurs in the education-to-employment space are solving for issues that will build the capacity of the future US workforce.

Employers agree that university students need more preparation to become ready for the workplace. This is evidenced by the high rate of voluntary turnover among new grads during their first year of employment.

Job Ready's editorial charter is to explore the education-to-employment gap through root causes of this high rate of turnover.

Job Readiness in the AI-Augmented Workplace

The workplace is rapidly transforming into an AI-augmented reality, and it's crucial to prepare your workforce for the future. In our executive roundtables, we bring together senior leaders from organizations like Jobs for the Future, BCG U, KPMG, University of Phoenix, and Oracle to explore:

  • The challenges and opportunities in preparing early career talent for an AI-driven job market
  • How organizations are adapting their workforce development strategies
  • Practical solutions for bridging the skills gap in the modern workplace
  • The future of work and career readiness programs
