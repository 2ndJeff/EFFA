PROBLEM

The System Wasn't Built to Work for Everyone

For decades, tuition assistance was designed backwards:

  • Employees couldn't afford to pay upfront
  • Employers couldn't measure results
  • Universities couldn't scale partnerships without intermediaries taking huge cuts

The result? Billions wasted, millions missing out on opportunity — and a gap between learning and earning.

MISSION-DRIVEN

Our Mission

We're on a mission to reduce student debt and rebuild America's talent pipeline by making employer-funded tuition work — enabling real-time control and measurable ROI for employers, universities, and every learner who dreams of a better job and a better life.
